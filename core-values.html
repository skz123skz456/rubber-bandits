What Guides Us

Core Values

These principles guide how the Rubber Bandits design, collaborate, and compete throughout every season.

Systems Over Individuals

We design workflows, documentation, and leadership structures that ensure continuity beyond any single member or season. By prioritizing systems and shared knowledge, our team builds sustainable progress year after year.

Strategic Intentionality

Every design, programming, and resource decision begins with structured game analysis and long-term planning. This ensures our solutions are purposeful, efficient, and aligned with competition strategy.

Data-Validated Iterations

Subsystem iterations, autonomous routines, and performance improvements are driven by testing data, modeling, and measurable outcomes. Every prototype moves our robot closer to optimal performance.

FIRST Core Values

Discovery, Innovation, Impact, Inclusion, Teamwork, and Fun form the foundation of FIRST. These principles shape our culture and guide how we collaborate, learn, and compete as the Rubber Bandits.
